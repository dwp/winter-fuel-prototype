You qualify for a Winter Fuel Payment of £200

You qualify for £200 because you:

  • were born before 6 October 1954
  • do not live with anyone who qualifies

What happens next

Your payment will be made in November or December.

This will happen automatically, you do not need to make a claim.

Changes of circumstance

Your entitlement may be affected if your circumstances change, such as:

  • moving house or there is a change to who you live with
  • being in hospital for more than 52 weeks
  • moving into a care home
