You qualify for a Winter Fuel Payment of £200

You qualify for £200 because you were born before 6 October 1954 and get one of these benefits:

  • Pension Credit
  • income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA)
  • income-related Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Income Support

What happens next

Your payment will usually be made in November or December, but may take until the following 31 March.

This will happen automatically. You do not need to make a claim.

If you live with someone who also gets one of these benefits, only one of you will get the payment.

Changes of circumstance

If your circumstances change, it could affect what you are entitled to. For example:

  • moving house or there is a change to who you live with
  • being in hospital for more than a year
  • moving into a care home

You should tell us about any change of circumstances as soon as possible.
