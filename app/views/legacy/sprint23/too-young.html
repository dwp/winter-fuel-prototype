You are too young to get a Winter Fuel Payment

According to the information you have given us, you are too young to get a Winter Fuel Payment.

You qualify for a Winter Fuel Payment if you were born before 6 October 1954.

If you’re on a low income

You may be able to apply directly to your electricity supplier for help if you do not get the Guarantee Credit element of Pension Credit but:

  • your energy supplier is part of the scheme
  • you’re on a low income
  • you get certain means-tested benefits

To get the discount you’ll need to stay with your supplier until it’s paid.

For more information and how to apply
www.gov.uk/the-warm-home-discount-scheme/low-income
