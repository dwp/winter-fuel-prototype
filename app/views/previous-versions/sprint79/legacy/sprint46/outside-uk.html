Because you live outside the UK

Not everyone who lives outside the UK can get a Winter Fuel Payment.

You might still get the payment if you live in the EU, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland or Liechtenstein.

You need to have a genuine and sufficient link to the UK - this can include having lived or worked in the UK, and having family in the UK.

You cannot get the payment if you live in Cyprus, France, Gibraltar, Greece, Malta, Portugal or Spain because the average winter temperature there is higher than the warmest region of the UK.

Make a claim

Make a claim by calling the Winter Fuel Payment Centre.
