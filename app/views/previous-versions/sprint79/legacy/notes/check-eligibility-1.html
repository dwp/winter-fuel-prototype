Research notes

  • Residency screen with ‘will you be…’ questions was deemed odd & difficult for people to respond to as most cannot predict future. We’ve consistently seen this in other research sessions too, so want to revise how to approach this. Hospital in particular only matters if more than 52 weeks
  • Deferred state pension content on start page - unnecessary for most users, and also was news to most. We want to re-think how to place this content so it’s only relevant for those that need it
  • Some advice from SME that we need to review over 80 DOB against Q-week to ensure we don’t incorrectly advise people in the eligiblity tool
