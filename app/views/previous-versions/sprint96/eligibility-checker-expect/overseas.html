You might still qualify for a Winter Fuel Payment

You could still get the payment if you live in one of these countries:

Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, Germany, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Sweden and Switzerland.

You will also need to have a genuine and sufficient link to the UK - this can include having lived or worked in the UK, and having family in the UK.
